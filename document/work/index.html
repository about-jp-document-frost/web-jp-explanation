Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Q. What should I keep in mind during an interview?
    A. Being on time, giving a concise self-introduction, and clearly explaining your reasons for applying are especially important.
  2. Q. How should I begin a business email?
    A. In Japanese workplaces, starting with a polite greeting equivalent to “Thank you for your continued support” is very common.
  3. Q. How formal should clothing be?
    A. It depends on the field, but for interviews and first meetings, clean and modest clothing is usually the safest choice.
  4. Q. What is important in the workplace?
    A. Reporting, informing, and consulting (`hourensou`) are considered important for smooth teamwork.
